My name is Janine Kim.

I am moved everyday to improve the quality of life for those mired by unequal economic and educational opportunities.

I value kindness and strength, softness of heart and power- I strive to combine these four traits with grace. A lot of times I fail, but then I learn.

I learned empathy from books- J.K. Rowling and Emily Bronte epitomized my childhood. I am a proud Korean American, and a Catholic Christian.

My favorite color is blue- all shades of it, from charcoal ocean to barely there fluff. In fact, the blue at the top of this page is periwinkle blue. I love this color because it gives off happy yet calming vibes at the same time.

I like ddukbokki.
